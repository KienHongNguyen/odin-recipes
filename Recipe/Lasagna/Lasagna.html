Lasagna

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g can crushed tomatoes
  • 175g can tomato paste
  • 5ml (1 tsp) dried basil
  • 5ml (1 tsp) dried oregano
  • 5ml (1 tsp) salt
  • 1.25ml (1/4 tsp) black pepper

For the Cheese Mixture

  • 900g container ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 125g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 60ml (1/4 cup) chopped fresh parsley

Other Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 60ml (1/4 cup) grated Parmesan cheese
