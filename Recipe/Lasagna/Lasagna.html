Lasagna

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g can crushed tomatoes
  • 175g can tomato paste
  • 5ml (1 tsp) dried basil
  • 5ml (1 tsp) dried oregano
  • 5ml (1 tsp) salt
  • 1.25ml (1/4 tsp) black pepper

For the Cheese Mixture

  • 900g container ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 125g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 60ml (1/4 cup) chopped fresh parsley

Other Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 60ml (1/4 cup) grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Make the Meat Sauce: In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook the 500g ground beef and diced onions until beef is browned and onions are translucent. Drain excess fat. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant. Stir in 800g crushed tomatoes, 175g tomato paste, 5ml basil, 5ml oregano, 5ml salt and 1.25ml pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Make the Cheese Mixture: In a bowl, mix together 900g ricotta, 1 egg, 125g Parmesan and 60ml chopped parsley until well combined.
  3. Cook the Lasagna Noodles: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the 12 lasagna noodles for 6-8 minutes until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna: Preheat oven to 190Â°C. Spread 250ml of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 23x33cm baking dish. Arrange 3 noodles lengthwise over sauce. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over noodles, then 250ml meat sauce and 125g mozzarella. Repeat layers twice, ending with remaining meat sauce and mozzarella on top. Sprinkle with 60ml Parmesan.
  5. Bake: Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake 25 minutes more until hot and bubbly.
  6. Let Stand: Allow lasagna to stand 10-15 minutes before cutting into squares to serve.
